Who am I?

"Go into the world to do well, but more importantly go into the world to do good."

Hello! I'm George-Ann, nice to meet you (virtually) and I hope to meet you in person soon.

I am a firm believer that every problem can be solved by looking at the numbers and analyzing history.

In December 2016, I was a warded my degree; a Bachelor of Arts, magna cum laude in Economics from Pace University's New York City Campus. I walked in May 2017. During my years at Pace, I discovered a love for research and telling a story with data. With that love in mind, I took courses such as health economics, econometrics, and political economy, in order to sharpen my skills in both qualitative and quantitative research while becoming more knowledgeable in a myriad of topics from the Political Economy of War to Monetary Policy.

Alongside my academic activities, I was active in campus life and held the posts of Vice President and President of The National Society of Leadership and Success, Sigma Alpha Pi (Spring 2014-Fall 2014), and Professor's Assistant in the Economics Department. Alongside my course load in statistics, econometrics, political economy, and sociology, I took many courses in digital design and web coding. In fact, this website has been coded and designed by myself (like it?).

For more information about my academic and professional experience, feel free to peruse my resume.
